# 英语作文素材积累（持续更新）

## 一、 背景引入与现象描述 (Introduction & Background)
适用于文章开篇，用于交代时代背景、社会现状或核心议题。
- **1** 随着...的快速发展 | `with the rapid development of ...`
- **2** ...的显著提高/ 稳步增长 | `the remarkable improvement/ steady growth of ...`
- **3** 先进的科学技术 | `advanced science and technology`
- **4** 面临新的机遇和挑战 | `be faced with new opportunities and challenges`
- **7** 引起了广泛的公众关注 | `arouse wide public concern/ draw public attention`
- **9** 热烈的讨论/ 争论 | `a heated discussion/ debate`
- **10** 有争议性的问题 | `a controversial issue`
- **24** 复杂的社会现象 | `a complicated social phenomenon`
- **42** 可靠的信息源 | `a reliable source of information`
- **43** 宝贵的自然资源 | `valuable natural resources`
- **46** 在...的方方面面 | `in all aspects of ...`
- **49** 科技的飞速更新 | `the ever-accelerated updating of science and technology`
- **55** ...必然趋势 | `an irresistible trend of...`
- **56** 日益激烈的社会竞争 | `the increasingly fierce social competition`

## 二、 观点陈述与态度表达 (Opinion & Attitude)
用于引出大众观点、表明个人立场或进行学术性让步。
- **5** 人们普遍认为 | `It is commonly believed/ recognized that…`
- **8** 不可否认 | `It is undeniable that…/ There is no denying that…`
- **11** 完全不同的观点 | `a totally different argument`
- **12** 一些人…而另外一些人… | `Some people… while others…`
- **13** 就我而言/ 就个人而言 | `As far as I am concerned, / Personally,`
- **14** 就…达到绝对的一致 | `reach an absolute consensus on…`
- **15** 有充分的理由支持 | `be supported by sound reasons`
- **16** 双方的论点 | `argument on both sides`
- **37** 无可非议 | `blameless / beyond reproach`
- **39** 应当承认 | `Admittedly,`
- **50** 对这一问题持有不同态度 | `hold different attitudes towards this issue`
- **51** 支持前/后种观点的人 | `people / those in favor of the former/ latter opinion`
- **68** 对…观点因人而异 | `Views on …vary from person to person.`
- **85** 毫无疑问 | `Undoubtedly, / There is no doubt that…`

## 三、 利弊权衡与对比分析 (Advantages, Disadvantages & Comparison)
用于客观评估事物两面性、进行长短利益对比或事物替换。
- **22** ...远远大于... | `the ... far outweigh the ...`
- **57** 眼前利益 | `immediate interest/ short-term interest`
- **59** …有其自身的优缺点 | `… has its merits and demerits`
- **67** 有利有弊 | `Every coin has its two sides. No garden without weeds.`
- **79** 与…比较 | `in comparison with`
- **80** 相反 | `in contrast / on the contrary.`
- **81** 代替 | `replace/ substitute / take the place of`

## 四、 影响分析与因果推导 (Impact & Causality)
用于阐述事件的作用机制、社会效应或事物间的逻辑关联。
- **6** 社会发展的必然结果 | `the inevitable result of social development`
- **17** 发挥着日益重要的作用 | `play an increasingly important role in...`
- **18** 对...必不可少 | `be indispensable to`
- **21** 对...产生有利/不利的影响 | `exert positive/ negative effects on...`
- **23** 导致，引起 | `lead to/ give rise to/ contribute to/ result in`
- **33** 对...有益 | `be beneficial / conducive to`
- **62** 对...有害 | `do harm to / be harmful to/ be detrimental to`
- **74** 有直接/间接关系 | `be directly / indirectly related to...`
- **86** 增进相互了解 | `enhance/ promote mutual understanding`

## 五、 解决方案与行动倡议 (Solutions & Actions)
用于提出具体措施、倡导社会行动或强调实践导向。
- **30** 考虑到诸多因素 | `take many factors into account/ consideration`
- **32** 做出共同努力 | `make joint efforts`
- **38** 致力于/ 投身于 | `be committed / devoted to…`
- **52** 有/ 提供如下理由/ 证据 | `have/ provide the following reasons/ evidence`
- **54** 理论和实践相结合 | `integrate theory with practice`
- **60** 扬长避短 | `Exploit to the full one’s favorable conditions and avoid unfavorable ones`
- **61** 取其精髓，取其糟粕 | `Take the essence and discard the dregs.`
- **63** 交流思想/ 情感/ 信息 | `exchange ideas/ emotions/ information`
- **64** 跟上...的最新发展 | `keep pace with / catch up with/ keep abreast with the latest development of ...`
- **65** 采取有效措施来... | `take effective measures to do sth.`
- **69** 重视 | `attach great importance to ...`
- **71** 把时间和精力放在...上 | `focus time and energy on ...`
- **75** 提出折中提议 | `set forth a compromise proposal`
- **77** 缓解压力/ 减轻负担 | `relieve stress/ burden`
- **78** 优先考虑/发展… | `give (top) priority to sth.`
- **87** 充分利用 | `make full use of / take advantage of`
- **90** 更多地强调 | `put more emphasis on...`
- **91** 适应社会发展 | `adapt oneself to the development of society`

## 六、 逻辑衔接与篇章结构 (Logical Connectors & Structure)
用于段落过渡、论点排序及层次推进，提升文章连贯性。
- **31** 从另一个角度 | `from another perspective`
- **53** 在一定程度上 | `to some extent/ degree / in some way`
- **93** 主要理由列举如下 | `The main reasons are listed as follows:`
- **94** 首先 | `First, / Firstly, /In the first place, /To begin with ...`
- **95** 其次 | `Second, / Secondly, / In the second place`
- **96** 再次 | `Besides / In addition, / Additionally, / Moreover, / Furthermore`
- **97** 最后 | `Finally, / Last but not the least, / Above all, / Lastly,`
- **98** 总而言之 | `All in all, / To sum up, / In summary, / In a word,`

## 七、 核心价值与个人发展 (Values & Personal Development)
聚焦个体素养提升、社会责任担当及宏观发展目标。
- **25** 责任感 / 成就感 | `sense of responsibility/ sense of achievement`
- **26** 竞争与合作精神 | `sense of competition and cooperation`
- **27** 开阔眼界 | `widen one’s horizon/ broaden one’s vision`
- **28** 学习知识和技能 | `acquire knowledge and skills`
- **29** 经济/心理负担 | `financial burden / psychological burden`
- **34** 为社会做贡献 | `make contributions to the society`
- **35** 打下坚实的基础 | `lay a solid foundation for`
- **36** 综合素质 | `comprehensive quality`
- **40** 不可推卸的义务 | `unshakable duty`
- **41** 满足需求 | `satisfy/ meet the needs of…`
- **48** 社会进步的体现 | `a symbol of society progress`
- **70** 社会地位 | `social status`
- **72** 扩大知识面 | `expand one’s scope of knowledge`
- **73** 身心两方面 | `both physically and mentally`
- **88** 承受更大的工作压力 | `suffer from heavier work pressure`
- **89** 保障社会的稳定和繁荣 | `guarantee the stability and prosperity of our society`
- **92** 实现梦想 | `realize one’s dream/ make one’s dream come true`
- **99** 我们还有很长的路要走 | `We still have a long way to go.`

## 八、 写作提示与高频替换 (Writing Tips & Lexical Substitution)
提供学术化词汇替换方案及句式引用技巧，避免表达单一化。
- **19** 正如谚语所说 | `As the proverb goes:`
- **20** ...也不例外 | `... be no exception`
- **76** 可以取代 “think”的词 | `believe / claim / maintain / argue / insist / hold the opinion / belief that`
- **82** 经不起推敲 | `cannot bear closer analysis / cannot hold water`
- **84** 社会进步的反映 | `mirror of social progress`
